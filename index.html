How to use FreeVoice

Talking

  • Tap a tile to hear it and add it to the sentence bar at the top.
  • Tap 🔊 Speak to read the whole sentence aloud.
  • Tap Clear to reset the sentence bar.
  • Tap a chip in the sentence bar to remove just that word.
  • Tap ⛶ to hide the browser bar (fullscreen) before handing the device over.

Adding tiles & categories (Parent Mode)

  • Tap ⚙ and enter the PIN (default 1234) to open Parent Mode.
  • Type a label, pick a part of speech (colors the tile), then either take/choose a photo or search for a symbol online.
  • Optionally record your own voice for that tile, then tap Save tile — it's placed in the next open grid slot automatically.
  • Add new categories (e.g. "Kitchen," "School") from the Categories section, and switch between them anytime from the tab bar.

Rearranging & reports

  • Rearrange tiles: tap a tile, then tap where it should go. Positions stay fixed otherwise, so they become muscle memory over time.
  • Usage reports: see total taps, taps this week, and top words — useful to share with a speech therapist. Exportable as a CSV.

Backup & sharing boards

  • Export backup (.json): everything — use this for your own backups and to restore later.
  • Export board (.obf): just the current category, in a format other AAC apps understand — use this to share a single board with someone else.
  • Print this category: a paper version of the current board, for when a device isn't available.
Installing on a phone/tablet & locking it to this app
  • Open this page in the device's browser, then use the browser menu → Add to Home Screen.
  • Android: Settings → Security → Screen Pinning → turn on, then pin FreeVoice from the recent-apps view.
  • iOS: Settings → Accessibility → Guided Access → turn on, then triple-click the side button while FreeVoice is open.
  • Already installed and stuck in landscape? That was a bug (now fixed) — remove the home screen icon and re-add it, since orientation gets baked in at install time and won't update on its own.
